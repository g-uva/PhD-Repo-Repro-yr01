# Current research direction

**As of 2026-07-13**, the research direction is focused on **GPU telemetry, observability, and probing approaches for characterising AI workloads**.

This direction includes investigating how runtime and hardware signals can be collected, related, and interpreted to describe AI workload behaviour across models, frameworks, operators, and accelerator execution. The emphasis is on reproducible measurement methods and on connecting telemetry sources, probing mechanisms, experimental configurations, and observed results through explicit provenance.

This is a living research-direction statement rather than a claim about the scope or conclusions of every catalogued paper. It should be updated and re-dated when the direction materially changes.
